## scotty at cmu - school power specs

### home base (the cut)
- starter ability: zoomies
- early unlock: territory (quick tutorial unlock)
- purpose: teach movement + survival loop before school missions

### hamerschlag hall - college of engineering
- mission: reactivate 3 power relays in order
- unlock power: `coil turret`
- coil turret effect: auto-deploy a mini turret every 18s for 8s, fires at nearest goop
- passive bonus: +12% projectile speed
- helper support: engineering crew repairs turret faster (+15% turret fire rate after first unlock)

### gates hillman complex - school of computer science
- mission: route a signal path through broken nodes
- unlock power: `targeting patch`
- targeting patch effect: every 14s, scotty gets 4s smart lock (faster retarget + slight aim assist)
- passive bonus: +90 attack range
- helper support: cs students optimize code path (+10% base fire rate)

### wean hall - physics + cs crossover
- mission: stabilize 3 oscillating field anchors (timing windows)
- unlock power: `repel field`
- repel field effect: pulse knockback ring every 16s, pushes nearby goops and interrupts contact
- passive bonus: -10% contact damage taken
- helper support: physics lab techs tune field (+20% repel radius)

### mellon institute - chemistry
- mission: complete safe reagent sequence
- unlock power: `catalyst mix`
- catalyst mix effect: territory puddles apply extra burst tick when goops enter
- passive bonus: +20% territory dot and +10 territory radius
- helper support: chem staff supply stabilizer (+1.5s territory duration)

### baker hall - psychology
- mission: calm panic waves with short timing prompts
- unlock power: `focus bark`
- focus bark effect: every 13s, a bark pulse fears nearby goops for 1.2s and reduces pressure
- passive bonus: -12% incoming panic/contact chip damage
- helper support: psych students coordinate evac lanes (+10% ally response speed)

## final boss setup - goop core
- boss name: `the dean of goop`
- gate rule: boss becomes realistically beatable only after 3+ school unlocks
- full strength route: clear all 5 schools for complete support stack
- boss behavior notes:
  - phase 1: sends goop waves
  - phase 2: arena puddle pressure
  - phase 3: aggressive rush + add spawns
